Program Schedule

The Pre-College Program runs throughout the academic year, offering regular sessions in chamber music, masterclasses, and performance classes. Detailed schedules are provided to enrolled students at the beginning of each term.

Students participate in weekly chamber coaching sessions, with performances scheduled at the end of each term and participation in the Kiwanis Music Festival. Masterclasses and performance classes are scheduled regularly throughout the year.
