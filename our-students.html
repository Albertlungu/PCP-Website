Our Students

Albert Lungu

Albert Lungu is a grade 10 Merivale pre-IB student with a decade-long passion for the study of violin. He has been a member of the Ottawa Youth Orchestra Academy (OYOA), showcasing his skills both in the Concert (2021-22) and Symphony (2023-24) orchestra. More recently, Albert has expanded his interests for string instruments to include viola, and he is currently studying both violin and viola with Michael Van der Sloot. His love for music has led him to study oboe in high school and perform in the school band. Albert looks forward to returning in the next school year to the OYOA as first violonist in the Symphony orchestra, and is excited to join the Pre-College Program at the University of Ottawa as a violist. Beyond music, Albert enjoys scuba diving, biking and programming, as well as many other activities and sports.
