Our Students

Jacob Kang

Jacob has played the cello for 7 years. He has competed 6 times in the Kiwanis Music Festival, where he has been awarded the Richard Casselman Trophy (2019), Pawel Szymczyk Marjanovic trophy (2023 and 2024), and several scholarships, including the Pomer Family Scholarship of $1000. He has also participated in the Ontario Music Festival, achieving Honourable Mention in 2021, 3rd in 2022 and 2nd twice in 2023 and 2024. Aside from solo contests, Jacob has been a member of the Ottawa Youth Symphony Orchestra since 2022, and performed with the Thirteen Strings Chamber Orchestra under the Camerata Junior Strings programme in 2019. In addition, Jacob won first place in the Ontario Music Festival as principal cellist of I Cellisti. As a member of Capital Strings and Voices Collective, he has contributed to the community by playing chamber music at retirement homes, performing at events (Dragon Boat Festival, Migratory Bird Day, Children's Festival, etc.), and teaching children music at the Kitigan Zibi First Nations Reserve. Jacob recently performed with the Cappies Orchestra at NAC in June and has been asked to play at the Rising Stars Chamberfest in July.

Philippe Lafleur

Philippe Lafleur is 16 years old and is going into grade 11 at De La Salle high school this September. He has been taking piano and violin lessons since elementary school and is part of the Ottawa Youth Symphony Orchestra. Philippe won the Edythe Young Browne Memorial Trophy (for senior strings solo) and received the John Gazsi Memorial Scholarship for his violin performances at the 2024 Ottawa Kiwanis Music Festival. Philippe has participated in the uOttawa Summer String Academy, the Music at Port Milford Academy, and the Domaine Forget de Charlevoix International Music and Dance Academy. Philippe also likes to spend time with family and friends, play video games, and read books like The Hunger Games.
