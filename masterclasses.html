Masterclasses

Learn from the Masters

Masterclasses are an excellent opportunity to practice performing while gaining input from external instructors. Guest artists for these classes will come from the University of Ottawa as well as the surrounding region. Masterclasses will be given on violin (8), viola (4), and cello (4).

♪

Violin Masterclasses

Eight masterclasses throughout the year with distinguished violin instructors.

♫

Viola Masterclasses

Four specialized masterclasses focusing on viola technique and repertoire.

♬

Cello Masterclasses

Four masterclasses with expert cellists from the region and university.

What to Expect

  • Perform for distinguished guest artists and receive personalized feedback
  • Learn from instructors at the University of Ottawa and the surrounding region
  • Gain insights into interpretation, technique, and musical expression
  • Observe and learn from your peers' performances and feedback
  • Build confidence in public performance settings
  • Expand your understanding of repertoire and performance practice
