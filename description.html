Program Description

About the Program

The Pre-College Program at the University of Ottawa is designed for advanced music students attending high school in the Ottawa/Gatineau area. Students will enjoy pursuing their musical potential through chamber music, masterclasses, and performance classes. The program is designed for serious students who may be considering a career in music. A minimum of RCM Grade 9 level is required.

The purpose of the Pre-College Program is to supplement a student's regular private instruction, not replace it. Bringing together faculty from the University of Ottawa along with prominent instructors from the area, students will enjoy world-class instruction. Students from a variety of studios across the region will join to create a community unparalleled in the area!
