Performance Class

Perform and Grow Together

Performance classes are additional opportunities to perform in front of peers within the Pre-College Program. Students will give feedback to one another and support each other on the road to excellence!

♪

Regular Performance

Frequent opportunities to perform in a supportive, peer-focused environment.

♫

Peer Feedback

Learn to give and receive constructive feedback from fellow musicians.

♬

Supportive Community

Build confidence and support each other on the journey to musical excellence.

Benefits of Performance Class

  • Build performance confidence in a supportive environment
  • Develop critical listening and analytical skills
  • Learn to articulate musical ideas and provide constructive feedback
  • Practice performing under pressure in a low-stakes setting
  • Gain exposure to diverse repertoire through peer performances
  • Strengthen communication skills essential for professional musicians
  • Create a supportive network of fellow musicians
